Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vinyl Player free to use?

Yes! Vinyl Player is completely free to use. We support our service through advertisements.

Does Vinyl Player play real music?

Currently, Vinyl Player is a visual simulation of a vinyl record player. We're working on integrating actual audio playback in future updates.

What browsers are supported?

Vinyl Player works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Can I use Vinyl Player on mobile?

Absolutely! Our responsive design ensures a great experience on phones and tablets.
